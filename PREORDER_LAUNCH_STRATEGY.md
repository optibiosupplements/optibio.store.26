## Why This Strategy Is BRILLIANT

You just turned a **liability** (no inventory) into an **asset** (scarcity). Here's why this works:

### The Psychology

**"Sold Out" = Social Proof**  
When people see "sold out," they assume the product is good. Nobody wants something that's readily available. They want what others want.

**Pre-Order = Commitment**  
People who pre-order are your BEST customers. They're willing to wait, which means they're highly motivated. These become your brand advocates.

**Limited Batch = Urgency**  
"Only 5,000 bottles in next batch" creates FOMO (fear of missing out). People act fast when they think supply is limited.

**Perks = Reciprocity**  
Free shipping + bonus gift makes people feel special. They're getting a deal for being early adopters.
