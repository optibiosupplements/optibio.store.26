# Supplement E-commerce CRO Research (2025-2026)

## Key Findings from Latest Research

### High-Converting Landing Page Elements (Replo, Jan 2026)

1. **Ad-Aligned Headline**
   - Lead with benefit that echoes ad promise
   - Match headline to single clear goal
   - Keep subhead tight, support with strong hero image

2. **Trust-Building Social Proof**
   - Drop review quote near buy box
   - Add star ratings with real counts
   - Include recognizable logos
   - Short testimonial tied to benefit
   - Press or certification logos
   - Guarantee or return policy callout
   - Clear shipping expectations

3. **Benefit-First Copy and Imagery**
   - Show, then tell
   - Use photos/videos to answer common questions
   - Quality lifestyle product photos with context
   - Help customers envision product in their day-to-day life

4. **Fast Loading Mobile Experience**
   - Mobile is huge source of shopper traffic
   - After bad mobile experience, users 62% less likely to buy
   - Critical for conversion

5. **Clear Action-Oriented CTA**
   - Use specific verbs tied to outcome
   - Make clear where clicking leads
   - Place primary CTA above fold
   - Repeat at natural pauses

### 2026 E-commerce Trends

- Customer acquisition costs rose 44% YoY (Q4 2024 to Q4 2025)
- CRO is now mandatory, not optional
- Retailers doubling down on conversion optimization
- DTC brands focusing on full-funnel optimization

### Supplement-Specific Insights

- Ashwagandha market leaders win with high ratings and high reviews
- Clinical doses must be listed and verifiable against studies
- Trust and credibility signals critical for supplements
- Scientific backing and third-party testing are key differentiators

### Wellness Brand Design Trends 2026

- "Feel like a deep breath" - soft visuals, thoughtful typography
- Seamless user experience that invites trust
- Wellness products now mainstream, affordable, woven into everyday life
- Focus on battling burnout narrative
