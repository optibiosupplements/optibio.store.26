## Current Product Image Analysis

### Design Elements:

**Bottle:**
- Black glossy pharmaceutical-grade bottle
- Premium matte black finish with subtle botanical embossing pattern
- Gold metallic cap (brushed gold finish)
- Professional supplement bottle shape (wide mouth, straight sides)

**Label Design:**
- OptiBio logo at top (blue-to-gold gradient with leaf accent)
- "ASHWAGANDHA KSM-66" in gold serif typography
- "90 CAPSULES" in white bold sans-serif
- "300mg per capsule" in smaller white text
- Bottom badges: "NON GMO", "GMP CERTIFIED", "MADE IN USA" (gold circular badges)
- Subtle botanical pattern embossed on bottle (leaves, vines)

**Color Palette:**
- Deep black bottle (#000000 or very dark charcoal)
- Antique gold cap (#C9A961 - matches brand gold)
- Blue-to-gold gradient logo (matches brand identity)
- White text for contrast
- Gold accent elements

**Photography Style:**
- Clean white/ivory background (#F7F4EF - brand ivory)
- Professional product photography
- Centered composition
- Soft studio lighting (no harsh shadows)
- Slight reflection under bottle for depth

**Premium Indicators:**
- Pharmaceutical-grade appearance
- Gold metallic elements
- Quality certifications visible
- Clean, minimalist design
- Professional typography hierarchy
