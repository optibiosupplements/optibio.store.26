# OptiBio Branding Requirements

**Source:** OptiBio Brand Manual + Corrections Document

## Logo Specifications

### Master Logo
- **Primary Logo:** "optibio" wordmark with leaf icon integrated into the "o" of "bio"
- **Logo Variations:**
  - Full color version (blue gradient)
  - White version (for dark backgrounds)
  - Logo can be used over photos
- **Minimum Size:** 82.65px × 59.92px
- **Maximum Size:** No maximum defined

### Logo Icon
- Circular blue icon with white leaf symbol
- Can be used standalone for app icons, favicons, social media

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
1. **Deep Navy Blue**
   - RGB: 38/25/94
   - CMYK: 100/100/30/22
   - HEX: #a26235e

2. **Medium Blue**
   - RGB: 41/98/143
   - CMYK: 94/59/21/4
   - HEX: #29628f

3. **White**
   - RGB: 250/250/250
   - CMYK: 0/0/0/0
   - HEX: #FFFFFF

**Gradient:**
- Combination of deep navy (#a26235e) and medium blue (#29628f)
- Angle: 93.0941°

### Typography

**Headline Font:** Poppins Bold
- Used for: Headlines, product names, CTAs

**Body Copy Font:** Poppins Regular
- Used for: Body text, descriptions, general content
