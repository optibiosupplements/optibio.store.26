## Executive Summary

Based on comprehensive research of supplement e-commerce conversion strategies from the past six months (August 2025 – January 2026), I have developed three distinct landing page mockup designs for OptiBio's Ashwagandha KSM-66 supplement. Each concept takes a fundamentally different approach to conversion optimization while incorporating the latest CRO best practices from the health and wellness industry.

The global dietary supplements market is projected to reach $372.42 billion by 2030, growing at a compound annual growth rate of 9.1% [1]. Within this competitive landscape, landing page optimization has become critical—research shows that targeted landing pages achieve a median conversion rate of 6.6%, significantly outperforming typical e-commerce sites at 4.7% [2].

## Mockup A: "Clinical Authority"

### Design Philosophy

This concept positions OptiBio as the scientifically superior choice for discerning customers who research before purchasing. The dark, premium aesthetic communicates exclusivity and clinical credibility, appealing to buyers who value evidence-based wellness products.

### Hero Section Design

![Mockup A Hero Section](./images/mockup-a-clinical-authority.png)

### Key Design Elements

The hero section opens with a prominent gold banner stating "CLINICALLY STUDIED • 24 PEER-REVIEWED STUDIES" to immediately establish scientific authority. The dramatic product photography showcases the amber bottle surrounded by raw ashwagandha roots and leaves, connecting the supplement to its natural origins while maintaining a premium aesthetic.

The headline "THE SCIENCE OF CALM" positions the product at the intersection of scientific rigor and emotional benefit. The subtext reinforces the clinical credibility with "600mg KSM-66 Root Extract - The world's most clinically studied ashwagandha."

Price anchoring is prominent, with the pre-order price of $28.35 displayed in gold against the struck-through original price of $69.99, accompanied by a "59% OFF" badge. The gold "PRE-ORDER NOW" button maintains visual consistency with the premium aesthetic.

Trust badges at the bottom reinforce key differentiators: 5% Withanolides (potency), 90-Day Money Back (risk reversal), 3rd Party Tested (quality assurance), and Non-GMO Organic (purity).

### Full Page Layout

![Mockup A Full Page](./images/mockup-a-full-page.png)

### Below-Fold Sections

The full page design continues the clinical authority theme with sections including "The KSM-66 Difference" featuring clinical study results with specific percentages (69% stress reduction, 72% sleep improvement, 64% focus boost), customer testimonials in premium dark cards, a "How It Works" three-step guide, and an FAQ accordion. The page concludes with a final CTA section and dark footer.

### Best Suited For

Customers who conduct extensive research before purchasing supplements, value scientific backing, and are willing to pay premium prices for clinically validated products.

## Mockup B: "Lifestyle Wellness"

### Design Philosophy

This concept appeals to wellness-oriented customers seeking a daily ritual that fits naturally into their lifestyle. The warm, inviting aesthetic creates an aspirational connection, positioning the supplement as part of a calm, balanced life rather than just a product to purchase.

### Hero Section Design

![Mockup B Hero Section](./images/mockup-b-lifestyle-wellness.png)

### Key Design Elements

The announcement bar opens with the offer "PRE-ORDER SPECIAL: 59% OFF + FREE SHIPPING" in a soft gold tone that doesn't feel aggressive. The elegant serif headline "Feel calm again. Think clearly. Sleep deeply." speaks directly to emotional outcomes rather than product features.

The lifestyle hero image shows the product in a cozy morning scene with steaming tea, spa stones, and natural light streaming through a window. This creates an aspirational vision of the calm, balanced life the customer desires.

A single testimonial card ("Changed my life - I finally sleep through the night - Sarah M., Verified Buyer") creates personal connection and social proof without overwhelming the design. The soft gold "START YOUR RITUAL" button uses benefit-focused language rather than transactional terms.

The social proof footer "12,000+ people have found their calm" with avatar images creates community belonging and reduces purchase anxiety.

### Full Page Layout

![Mockup B Full Page](./images/mockup-b-full-page.png)

### Below-Fold Sections

The full page continues the warm wellness theme with "Your Daily Calm in 3 Steps" featuring illustrated icons (morning coffee, midday sun, evening moon), "Real Transformations" showing before/after stress level visualizations, "Ancient Wisdom, Modern Science" with botanical illustrations of ashwagandha, an Instagram-style UGC gallery, and a "Subscribe & Save" section. The page concludes with "JOIN THE CALM COMMUNITY" CTA.

### Best Suited For

Wellness-focused customers who respond to emotional messaging, value community and shared experiences, and are looking for products that integrate into their daily self-care routines.

## Mockup C: "Clean Minimalist"

### Design Philosophy

This concept prioritizes clarity and efficiency for customers who want to quickly understand the product's value and make a purchase decision. The clean, modern aesthetic removes friction and presents information in a scannable, organized format.

### Hero Section Design

![Mockup C Hero Section](./images/mockup-c-clean-minimalist.png)

### Key Design Elements

A sticky header provides clear navigation (Shop, Science, Reviews) with a cart icon. The urgency countdown timer "PRE-ORDER ENDS IN: 02d 14h 32m" creates time pressure without being overly aggressive.

The two-column layout places the clean product shot on the left with thumbnail navigation, while the right column presents information in a highly scannable format. The bold "ASHWAGANDHA KSM-66" headline with "Premium Root Extract" subtext immediately identifies the product.

A benefit checklist with gold checkmarks allows quick scanning: Reduce stress & anxiety, Improve sleep quality, Boost mental clarity, Support physical recovery. The pricing section shows "$28.35 / bottle" with clear savings information.

Quantity selector buttons [1] [2] [3] encourage multi-bottle purchases. The black "ADD TO CART" button provides strong visual contrast. The compact trust badges grid (Clinically Studied, 3rd Party Tested, Non-GMO & Organic, Made in USA) and shipping information complete the above-fold experience.

### Full Page Layout

![Mockup C Full Page](./images/mockup-c-full-page.png)

### Below-Fold Sections

The full page continues the minimalist theme with "Why KSM-66?" comparison table (Generic vs. KSM-66), "Clinical Results" data visualization cards, "Customer Reviews" carousel with filter buttons, "As Seen In" press logos, and "Bundle & Save" pricing tiers. A sticky footer bar with product image, price, and CTA ensures the purchase option is always accessible.

### Best Suited For

Efficiency-focused customers who want to quickly evaluate a product, compare options, and make a purchase decision without extensive scrolling or emotional engagement.

## Recommendations

### For A/B Testing

I recommend implementing Mockup A (Clinical Authority) and Mockup B (Lifestyle Wellness) as the initial A/B test, as they represent the most distinct approaches and target different customer segments. Mockup C can be tested subsequently against the winner.

### For Single Implementation

If only one design will be implemented initially, I recommend **Mockup B (Lifestyle Wellness)** for the following reasons:

1. The emotional messaging aligns with current wellness culture trends emphasizing self-care and burnout prevention
2. The warm aesthetic differentiates OptiBio from competitors using clinical/sterile designs
3. The lifestyle imagery creates stronger aspirational connection
4. The testimonial-forward approach builds trust effectively for new brands
