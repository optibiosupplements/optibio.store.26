# KSM-66 Ashwagandha Clinical Research - Compliant Marketing Claims

## Peer-Reviewed Clinical Studies

### Stress & Anxiety
- **Chandrasekhar et al. (2012)** - 834 citations
  - Double-blind, placebo-controlled study
  - Significant reduction in stress scores
  - Safe with no adverse effects on vital signs
  - Published in Indian Journal of Psychological Medicine

### Sleep Quality
- **Cheah et al. (2021)** - 151 citations
  - Meta-analysis of KSM-66 studies
  - Small but significant improvement in sleep quality vs. placebo
  - Published in PLOS ONE

### Physical Performance
- **Tiwari et al. (2021)** - 73 citations
  - Improved cardiorespiratory endurance
  - Enhanced physical performance metrics
  - Published in Journal of Ethnopharmacology

### Long-Term Safety
- **Salve et al. (2025)**
  - 12-month safety study
  - No deleterious effects on hepatic, renal function
  - Confirms long-term safety profile
  - Published in Phytotherapy Research

### Mental & Physical Conditions
- **Lopresti & Smith (2021)** - 73 citations
  - Systematic review of human trials
  - Alleviation of stress and anxiety symptoms
  - Enhancement of mental and physical conditions
  - Published in Journal of Herbal Medicine
